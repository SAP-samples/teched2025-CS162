# CS162 - Data-driven transition guidance toward the cloud

## Overview
<p align="justify">This hands-on session shows how to execute a <b>Selective Data Transition</b> using <b>SAP Business Transformation Center</b> (BTC). You will perform a guided migration from <b>SAP ERP 6.0</b> to <b>SAP Cloud ERP Private</b>, focusing on transferring only the data that remains relevant for future business operations.<p>

<p align="justify">Using <b>SAP Business Transformation Center</b>, you will analyze the <b>source system</b>, <b>define your transition scope</b>, and <b>execute the migration</b> in a controlled and data-driven way.</p>

![](./resources/images/Screenshot-01.png)<br><br>

## Requirements
To complete the exercises in this hands-on session, you need **SAP GUI** and a **web browser** (preferably **Google Chrome**).<br><br>

## Exercises
<p align="justify">Building on the prepared landscape, the exercises guide you through <b>each phase</b> of the transition â€” from <b>scoping</b> and <b>modeling</b> to <b>executing</b> and <b>monitoring</b> the results in the target system.</p>

<p align="justify">Step by step, you will refine your <b>transition scope</b>, create a <b>digital blueprint</b>, and perform a <b>complete migration run</b>, validating business data directly in the target system.</p>
